[Rocket.Chat](https://rocket.chat) is an open-source, secure, fully customizable communications platform developed in TypeScript for organizations with high standards of data protection.

We enable real-time conversations between colleagues, other companies, and your customers or citizens, regardless of how they connect. Every day, tens of millions of users in over 150 countries trust Rocket.Chat to keep their communications private and secure.

---

## 🚀 Platform Overview

Rocket.Chat provides endless possibilities for secure collaboration:

*   💬 [**Team Collaboration**](https://docs.rocket.chat/docs/collaborate-using-rocketchat): A single point for secure internal and cross-company collaboration.
*   🎯 [**Omnichannel Engagement**](https://docs.rocket.chat/docs/omnichannel): Seamless interactions across platforms like WhatsApp, SMS, and more.
*   🦾 [**Optimization**](https://docs.rocket.chat/docs/optimize-your-workspace): Enhance productivity with self-hosted AI and a customizable workspace.
*   🤖 [**Extend and Integrate**](https://docs.rocket.chat/docs/extend-and-integrate-rocketchat-capabilities): Build your own apps or embed the workspace into your platform.

## 🛠️ Development Requirements

To set up Rocket.Chat locally, ensure your environment meets these specifications:

*   **Node.js:** v22.16.0 or higher
*   **Deno:** Latest stable version
*   **Package Manager:** Yarn (recommended)

## 💻 Deploy Rocket.Chat

Choose the method that best fits your needs:

*   **Self-Managed:** Deploy using [Docker](https://docs.rocket.chat/docs/deploy-rocketchat), [Podman](https://docs.rocket.chat/docs/deploy-rocketchat), or [Kubernetes](https://docs.rocket.chat/docs/deploy-rocketchat). Check the [System Requirements](https://docs.rocket.chat/docs/system-requirements) first.
*   **Air-Gapped:** Set up an [isolated workspace](https://docs.rocket.chat/docs/rocketchat-air-gapped-deployment) for high-security environments.
*   **Cloud Hosted:** Use our premium, dedicated [cloud hosting options](https://docs.rocket.chat/docs/rocketchat-cloud-hosting-service-level-agreement-sla).
*   **Federation:** Enable [decentralized communication](https://docs.rocket.chat/docs/rocketchat-native-federation) across networks.
